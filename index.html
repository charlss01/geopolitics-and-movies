SNOWPIERCER IS AN ALLEGORY OF CLASS CULTURE AND HOW TENSIONS INNATE WITHIN LEAD TO RESENTMENT, REBELLION, AND EVENTUALLY, CHANGE OR REVOLUTION. In the series, this allegory is shown through the division of a train by social classes, the train symbolising the global structure of POWER. The rich live in the front cars, surrounded by luxury, while the poor are confined to the tail surviving in miserable conditions.
